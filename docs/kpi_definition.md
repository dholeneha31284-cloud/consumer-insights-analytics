# Key Performance Indicators

## Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

Measures the average satisfaction rating provided by customers.

---

## Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Measures the likelihood of customers recommending the brand to others.

---

## Brand Awareness

Percentage of respondents who recognize the brand.

---

## Brand Recall

Percentage of respondents who can recall the brand without assistance.

---

## Brand Preference

Percentage of respondents selecting the brand as their preferred choice.

---

## Purchase Intent

Distribution of customer willingness to purchase in the future.

---

## Campaign Reach

Percentage of customers exposed to marketing campaigns.

---

## Campaign Response Rate

Distribution of positive, neutral, and negative responses following campaign exposure.
