# Business Problem

## Background

NovaMart Retail is a multi-city retail brand operating across India. The company conducts periodic customer surveys to understand customer experience, brand perception, and marketing campaign effectiveness.

Although large volumes of survey data are collected, the business lacks a structured analytical framework to transform this information into actionable insights. As a result, decision-makers find it difficult to identify customer needs, evaluate campaign performance, and prioritize improvement initiatives.

## Problem Statement

The Customer Insights team requires an analytics solution that consolidates survey responses and customer information into meaningful business insights.

The solution should enable the organization to:

* Understand customer satisfaction levels.
* Measure brand awareness and brand preference.
* Evaluate marketing campaign effectiveness.
* Identify customer segments with similar characteristics.
* Discover factors influencing customer loyalty and purchase intent.
* Support data-driven business decisions through interactive dashboards and statistical analysis.

## Expected Outcome

The project will deliver an end-to-end analytics workflow that transforms raw survey data into business insights using SQL, Python, statistical analysis, and Power BI.
