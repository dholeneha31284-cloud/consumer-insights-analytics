# Sprint 1 – Project Setup & Dataset Preparation

## Sprint Objective

Establish the project foundation by creating the repository structure, defining the business problem, preparing project documentation, and generating synthetic datasets for analysis.

---

# Repository Structure

The project repository was organized into dedicated folders for datasets, notebooks, SQL scripts, reports, dashboards, documentation, testing, and supporting scripts to ensure a clean and maintainable project structure.

---

# Dataset Preparation

Four related datasets were generated to simulate an enterprise customer survey environment.

### Customers

Contains customer demographic information including age, gender, city, education, occupation, income bracket, and customer segment.

### Survey Responses

Contains survey responses including brand awareness, brand preference, customer satisfaction, service quality, product quality, value for money, recommendation score, and purchase intent.

### Campaigns

Contains campaign master information including campaign identifier, campaign name, and communication channel.

### Campaign Exposure

Contains campaign exposure details and customer responses to marketing campaigns.
